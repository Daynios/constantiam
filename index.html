IVCO Public? ConstMe Almost Shutdown?

The Date is 7/21/2023! The IBM-Void Coalition officially went public today on their discord server, accepting members and more! So what is IVCO? IVCO was founded by IBMComputer and VoidJava on Constantiam.net in June 2023. A fairly new group, looking to have fun on Constantiam, it is mainly a building/grinding group, it's for those who want to chill and relax. Now for the second major story of today, Constantiam.me almost shutdown? Yes, it's true. In a poll taken yesterday, 71% of people voted to keep Constantiam.me, and 29% voted to keep it. The votes were finalized yesterday night, and Constantiam.me will continue to stay online as a community resource. Created, by IVCO. THe Future of Constantiam. https://discord.gg/QQyJQcFB2r
